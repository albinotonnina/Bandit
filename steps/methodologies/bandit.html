A Naming convention

from BEM

.UniqueIdentifier-componentName-variant

  1. UniqueIdentifier: camel case, capital or not. Long or short. Up to you.
  2. componentName (or whatever makes sense): camel case, preceeded by a hyphen.
  3. variant/state: lower case, optional, preceeded by a hyphen.

Apart from the unique identifier, and because of that, anything that follows may not have strict rules.

The key point: class names should communicate useful informations to developers.
